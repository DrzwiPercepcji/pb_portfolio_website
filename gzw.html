01

WHAT IS GDZIE ZJEŚĆ WEGE?

Gdzie Zjeść Wege is a restaurant search engine created
for customers with specific needs regarding their diet.
The user has access to quick filters defining the type
of theirs diet, and a map to help with navigation.

02

THE GOAL

My main goal was to create simple and intuitive website.
People don't like spending a lot of time searching, that's
why I decided to prototype it in a way where user needs
less then 20 seconds to find wanted restaurant.

03

THE PROBLEM

The biggest struggle was creating filtering options.
After many iterations, I prototyped most promising
solutions and tested them internally and also with
users.

04

THE SOLUTION

After several tests with users, the best solution turned
out to be leaving three main filters on the left side, just
above the list of restaurants, and placing advanced
filters in the slide-out menu. Thanks to this solution,
while selecting filters from the advanced menu, the list
of restaurants is constantly being updated on the map.
